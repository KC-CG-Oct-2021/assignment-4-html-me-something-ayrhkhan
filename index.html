embracing the learning process

my journey to find a learning process has been a journey in finding language for my needs and putting tension on the idea of knowing things and memorizing things. The tools that I have found have allowed me to the most access to intentional learning I've ever had.

anki

Anki is a space repetition software that uses the forgetting curve to help remember information.

My experience with Anki has been helpful in knowing what questions to ask to for better comprehension.

notion

Notion is a personal database software that allows for coding like techniques without coding.

My experience with Notion has helped me organize my thoughts and my time in a digestible way.
